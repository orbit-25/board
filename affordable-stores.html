🛒 Groceries & Essentials

Albert Heijn

As the largest supermarket chain in the Netherlands, Albert Heijn offers an extensive assortment from fresh produce and bakery goods to household essentials, premium and store brand items. Their Enschede Centrum location features in store pick-up and parcel services, plus bonus offers and loyalty perks. Expect high quality, seasonal selections, ready-to-eat meal options, international foods, and a dedicated service desk for returns, photo printing, and lottery tickets.

🗘️ View on Google Maps

Jumbo

Jumbo is the second largest supermarket chain in the Netherlands, known for its “lowest price guarantee”, large assortment, and fresh produce. This Enschede location (e.g. Noorderhagen or Dikkensweg store) combines in store shopping, pick-up, and delivery services. It stocks everyday staples, household goods, specialty items, and frequently updated deals on pasta, rice, snacks, and personal care. Customers report neat aisles and helpful staff, though during peak times some shelves may run low.

🗘️ View on Google Maps

Lidl

Lidl is known for its budget friendly prices and weekly rotating specials. Their Enschede store offers fresh meats, vegetables, bakery items, dairy, and pantry basics, plus seasonal non-food items such as kitchen gadgets, textiles, and tools. While the standard range is smaller than full service chains, Lidl delivers consistent quality at low cost, with promotional items often selling out quickly.

🗘️ View on Google Maps

Aldi

Aldi is another cost effective supermarket offering a no-frills shopping experience. Expect essentials like fresh produce, dairy, packaged food, and bakery goods. Their own-brand products prioritize simplicity and low price, and weekly specials may include seasonal groceries, household tools, or clothing basics. Availability is reliable but choose earlier hours for better stock.

🗘️ View on Google Maps

Kashmir Super Store – Gronau

Located just across the German border in Gronau, this store specializes in South Asian groceries. You’ll find a wide selection of halal spices, lentils, rice, snacks, frozen meats, and specialty sauces from India and Pakistan. Prices are competitive for ethnic products, and it's a popular destination for those seeking artisanal ingredients not typically found in Dutch supermarkets.

🗘️ View on Google Maps

Toko Het Oosten – Asian Supermarket

Excellent for Southeast and East Asian groceries: sauces, noodles, spices, fresh tofu, snacks, and frozen dumplings. Their shelves are well stocked, and you can often ask staff for cooking advice. A favorite for students and families seeking diverse Asian flavors.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Toko Switi – Asian & African Supermarket

Offers a unique blend of Asian and African foods: spices, teas, snacks, frozen goods, and halal meats. Known for friendly staff and authentic imports, including West African sauces, Caribbean spices, and Asian condiments.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Peter Hu Asian Supermarket

Wide selection of Asian and African ingredients: noodles, sauces, spices, frozen seafood, and ready to cook products. Regular restocking ensures availability of fresh ingredients and seasonal items.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Sam's Toko – Asian Supermarket

Convenient spot for Asian food staples, frozen goods, spices, snacks, and halal meats. Often praised for organized aisles and friendly service especially helpful if you're discovering new ingredients.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Togo Supermarkt en Levensmiddelen

Local budget supermarket specializing in halal meat cuts, spices, pickles, and fresh vegetables. Known for low-cost essentials and community-oriented service. Best visited earlier in the day for full selection.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Ada Supermarket

A small budget store offering Turkish sweets, halal meats, vegetables, spices, and pickles. Well-regarded for friendly owners and competitive prices on ethnic goods.

Ana Market

Die Grenze

Cross-border discount store offering a variety of food and non-food products at low prices. Some packaged food may have close expiration dates check labels carefully. Larger bargains found on household goods and snacks.

SPAR City Store

🏠 Home & Kitchen Supplies

Action – Budget Home & Living

Action is a fast-growing Dutch discount variety store with over 3,000 locations in Europe. The Enschede branch carries thousands of affordable products: home décor, stationery, seasonal decoration, DIY tools, cleaning supplies, personal care, small electronics, toys, and snacks. Many items are rotate‑stock specials, so you’ll find something new at low prices nearly every week. Ideal for essentials, craft supplies, and low-cost gadgets.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

HEMA – Everyday Essentials & More

HEMA Enschede (H.J. van Heekplein) offers a broad mix—from household goods and décor to clothing, toys, stationery, and bakery items. There’s also an in-store restaurant and takeaway corner serving sandwiches, rookworst, pea soup, cakes, and coffee. The store provides services like photo printing, ticket sales, and a self-scan checkout system. HEMA’s own-label products are known for clean design, fairness, and affordable quality. Frequent promotions and HEMA customer loyalty pass perks apply here.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

IKEA Hengelo – Furniture & Kitchenware

IKEA Hengelo is the regional destination for furniture, home organisation, kitchenware, and décor. The store includes free indoor/outdoor parking with EV charging, a Småland children’s play area, restaurant, and extensive customer services including kitchen planning (from €49.99 credited back on purchases over €500), interior advice, delivery, assembly, returns, spare parts, and van rental via Hertz. Second‑Chance Corner offers discounted returns. A well‑rounded spot for design, affordability, and sustainability

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Recycle Enschede Kringloop – Second-hand Home Goods

Recycle Enschede (Emmastraat 190) is a social‑enterprise thrift store open Monday through Saturday, offering used clothing, kitchenware, furniture, books and more. Handled with a focus on sustainability and social inclusion: they also provide home and business clearance services and paid work opportunities for people needing support. Expect changing inventory and good deals on vintage or gently used goods.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

👕 Clothing (Budget Options)

Primark – Budget Fashion & Essentials

This 5,555 m², three‑floor Primark at H.J. van Heekplein is one of the largest in the Netherlands. You’ll find affordable fashion across women’s, men’s, kids’, and homeware departments. Stock includes seasonal trends, basics, accessories, beauty, and home lifestyle items. The huge floor space and rotating inventory mean there’s virtually always something new—ideal for students or value shoppers.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Zara – Trendy Mid-Range Fashion

Zara Enschede spans approximately 4,000 m² over two floors. The ground level is dedicated to women’s fashion, while the first floor features men’s, children’s, baby wear, and home décor. Offers fast‑fashion collections in multiple price ranges and regular thematic drops. Services include app‑based “search & locate”, express pick‑up, self‑checkout, and in-store donation of used clothing to support local social projects.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

H&M – Affordable Fashion for All

H&M in Enschede offers a broad selection of trend-led apparel for women, men, teens, children, and plus-size. You’ll find seasonal capsules, sustainable-conscious lines like “Conscious Collection”, and basics at fair prices. Services typically include click‑and‑collect, garment recycling, and occasional in-store pop-ups or collaborations.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Kringloopwarenhuis Het Goed Enschede – Second-hand Clothing & More

This spacious ~1,000 m² thrift store offers a daily‑changing inventory: second‑hand clothing for women, men, children, books, toys, electronics, household goods, refurbished appliances, and furniture. All items are cleaned and quality‑checked. It supports a major Dutch social enterprise and even provides pick‑up service, donation drop‑off, and a small coffee corner. Great for sustainable shopping, with regular surprises and student‑friendly prices.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

🧼 Health & Beauty / Pharmacy

Kruidvat – Drugstore Chain

Kruidvat is one of the most widely available drugstore chains in the Netherlands. This store offers a mix of affordable personal care products, health essentials, toiletries, vitamins, makeup, and fragrances. In addition to A-brands, Kruidvat also features its own-label items known for competitive pricing. You can also find baby care items, hair styling tools, seasonal goods, and limited household electronics such as shavers, trimmers, or humidifiers. Some locations also have pharmacy counters for non-prescription consultations and click-and-collect service.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Etos – Beauty & Pharmacy

Etos specializes in wellness and pharmacy-related items, with a clean and modern layout that makes it easy to shop. The store stocks a wide range of skincare, makeup, vitamins, nutrition supplements, personal hygiene products, and family care essentials. Known for its customer service and stylish store design, Etos also carries its own brand, which is valued for quality and affordability. Staff are usually available to give product advice, especially in skincare and wellness segments.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

DA Drogist – Independent Pharmacy

DA Drogist is a more personalized drugstore experience, offering both popular and niche products in health, beauty, and wellness. The store includes a wide range of over-the-counter medications, natural remedies, vitamins, dermo-cosmetic brands, and grooming items. DA locations often emphasize service and advice, and in many cases, work in close cooperation with local general practitioners. Some stores also carry household products and small gifts, making them versatile neighborhood shops.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

💻 Electronics & Accessories

MediaMarkt – Consumer Electronics Superstore

MediaMarkt is one of the largest electronics retailers in Europe, offering a wide range of technology and home appliances. The Enschede branch features dedicated sections for laptops, smartphones, televisions, kitchen appliances, personal care gadgets, gaming consoles, printers, and accessories. Customers can explore both top-brand and budget-friendly options, including exclusive in-store discounts. Services include product consultation, extended warranties, on-site repairs, and in-store pick-up for online orders.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Phone House – SIM Cards & Mobile Accessories

Phone House is a mobile retail chain that specializes in SIM card activations, prepaid phone plans, mobile accessories, and budget smartphones. You can find major prepaid carriers like Lycamobile, Lebara, and Odido, alongside a wide range of mobile essentials such as power banks, chargers, screen protectors, and cases. Staff are helpful with number porting, plan upgrades, and device setup services, making it a reliable stop for communication needs.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Odido Shop – Phones & Data Plans

Odido (formerly T-Mobile Netherlands) operates its own brand store in Enschede, offering the latest smartphones, tablets, accessories, and flexible mobile contracts. The store provides postpaid and prepaid data plans, SIM card activations, eSIM support, and technical assistance for both individual and business customers. Staff can help with trade-ins, upgrades, and troubleshooting. It's ideal for those looking for Dutch mobile network services with multilingual support.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Action – Budget Tech Accessories

While primarily a discount store for household and lifestyle goods, Action also carries a budget-friendly selection of electronics and tech accessories. This includes USB cables, Bluetooth earbuds, LED lights, travel adapters, laptop sleeves, and power strips. Products are frequently rotated based on weekly deals, making it a great place to find low-cost gadgets or backups for everyday use.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

♻️ Second-Hand Stores

Kringloopwinkel RataPlan Enschede

RataPlan is one of the well-known sustainable second-hand chains in the Netherlands. Their Enschede store features a spacious layout filled with gently used furniture, clothing, books, homeware, and small electronics at low prices. The store promotes environmental awareness and circular economy by reusing goods that would otherwise go to waste. It also supports social employment by offering work opportunities to people distanced from the labor market. A great place to find vintage pieces, household basics, or unique upcycling projects.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Kringloop Enschede – Multiple Locations

Enschede hosts several kringloop (thrift) stores across the city, offering an ever-changing inventory of donated goods. These shops typically stock used furniture, clothing, toys, books, kitchenware, and bicycles at affordable prices. In addition to being budget-friendly, shopping second-hand here is environmentally responsible and supports local social work initiatives. Each store has its own focus — some are more curated, while others are larger warehouses with warehouse-style browsing. Visiting more than one can uncover hidden treasures.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

🚲 Bicycle Shops

Decathlon – Bikes & Outdoor Sports

Decathlon is a popular sporting goods chain that offers a wide range of affordable bicycles, gear, and accessories. Whether you're looking for city bikes, mountain bikes, foldable models, or electric bikes, Decathlon provides great value for students and commuters. In addition to new bikes, they offer repair kits, lights, helmets, and essential maintenance tools. Their in-house brands are known for their reliability and budget pricing, and basic repair services are also available at most locations.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

La Souris Enschede – Scooters & Bikes

La Souris is a nationwide scooter and e-bike retailer with a branch in Enschede. They offer stylish electric scooters, electric bicycles, and traditional bikes, along with financing and warranty services. Ideal for students and urban commuters, the store also has occasional second-hand or showroom models at discounted rates. Repairs and spare parts are handled on-site, and customers often benefit from seasonal promotions and bundle deals.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Bike Supply – Sales & Repairs

Bike Supply is a local bike shop offering a wide selection of new bicycles, ranging from basic city bikes to e-bikes and cargo options. They also specialize in repair services such as brake adjustments, flat tire fixes, and chain replacements. The store caters to both casual and daily riders, and often provides fast service for urgent repairs. Accessories like locks, baskets, and lighting kits are also available for purchase.

💇 Haircuts & Grooming

Ghanahouse – Barber & Grooming

Ghanahouse is a locally known grooming spot in Enschede offering stylish haircuts, clean fades, and beard trims at student-friendly prices. Popular among international students, this barbershop caters especially well to African hairstyles and textured hair. The environment is relaxed, and appointments are not always necessary, making it a convenient choice for a walk-in trim or quick freshen-up before class or a night out.

FA YI Hair Salon offers affordable grooming for both men and women, with services ranging from haircuts to styling and beard grooming. AMI Kappers Enschede – Hair Salon

AMI Kappers is a modern hair salon located in the city center, offering haircuts, coloring, styling, and grooming for all hair types. It’s part of a reputable Dutch chain known for stylish cuts and professional haircare products. The salon operates by appointment, and services are provided by trained stylists who offer personalized advice based on hair type and preference.

Located near the Sports Centre at the University of Twente, this student-run hair salon is a convenient and affordable option for students living on or near campus. Bagels & Beans is a warm and inviting café that offers a great selection of fresh bagels, healthy juices, and top-notch coffee. Known for its cozy ambiance, soft lighting, and mellow background music, it’s an ideal spot for solo study sessions or casual meetups. Free Wi-Fi and comfortable seating make it a student favorite for working on laptops without distractions.

Coffee Fellows

Located in the heart of Enschede, Coffee Fellows is a well-known chain café offering a modern environment with excellent coffee, bagels, snacks, and strong Wi-Fi. Bibliotheek Enschede

The central public library in Enschede provides a peaceful and well-equipped space for students looking for quiet study zones. Ideal for deep focus, this is a go-to place for those who prefer silence and structure over the more social vibe of cafés. Membership also offers access to e-books and academic resources.

🕌 Prayer & Worship Places

Enschede Mosque – Ulu Camii

Ulu Camii is one of the largest mosques in Enschede, located near Volkspark. It serves the Moroccan-Turkish community and is open for all five daily prayers as well as Friday (Jummah) prayers. The mosque features a clean and spacious prayer hall, a designated area for women, and community rooms for events, language lessons, and youth gatherings. It's a welcoming place for spiritual reflection and community involvement.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Grote Kerk – Protestant Church

Located at the heart of Enschede’s old market square, Grote Kerk is a historic Protestant church known for its welcoming atmosphere and impressive architecture. Regular Sunday services are held in Dutch, and visitors are always welcome to attend. Beyond worship, the church often hosts cultural events, concerts, and exhibitions, creating a meaningful space for both spiritual and community engagement.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

🌳 Parks & Picnic Spots

Volkspark

Located near the city center, Volkspark is one of Enschede’s most popular parks. It features large open lawns, shaded walking paths, playgrounds, a small pond, and barbecue areas perfect for group outings or solo relaxation. It's ideal for a casual picnic, outdoor sports, or simply a peaceful stroll amidst trees and sculptures.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Wooldrikspark

Wooldrikspark is a peaceful urban park offering a modern and artistic touch to nature. With well-maintained walking trails, small streams, grassy zones, and some public art installations, it provides a great backdrop for studying, meditating, or casual get-togethers. Less crowded than Volkspark, it’s perfect for some quiet time outdoors.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Rutbeek Recreatiepark

A large recreational lake on the outskirts of Enschede, Rutbeek offers swimming, sunbathing, picnicking, and water sports. With clean beaches, designated BBQ spots, walking trails, and ample grassy areas, it's ideal for both relaxing weekends and adventurous outings. It’s also a favorite location for summer festivals and outdoor events.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

📚 Libraries & Study Zones

Bibliotheek Enschede

The main public library in Enschede offers calm and modern spaces for studying, reading, or working. With free Wi-Fi, comfortable desks, and quiet zones, it’s perfect for students and remote workers. The building also houses digital services, Dutch language resources, and community events.

🗺️ View on Google Maps

Vrijhof - UT Library

Located on the University of Twente campus, Vrijhof offers silent study rooms, individual desks, and collaborative zones. Silent study areas must be reserved online in advance, but many desks are open for walk-ins. It’s a preferred choice for students needing a focused academic environment.

🗺️ View on Google Maps
