Albert Heijn

As the largest supermarket chain in the Netherlands, Albert Heijn offers an extensive assortment from fresh produce and bakery goods to household essentials, premium and store brand items. Their Enschede Centrum location features in store pick-up and parcel services, plus bonus offers and loyalty perks. Expect high quality, seasonal selections, ready-to-eat meal options, international foods, and a dedicated service desk for returns, photo printing, and lottery tickets.

Lidl is known for its budget friendly prices and weekly rotating specials.
